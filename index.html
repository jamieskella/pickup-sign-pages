Questions? Answered.

How does PickUp Sign work?

Open the app, type a name, and tap Show. Your phone becomes a full-screen sign with the name displayed as large as possible. The screen stays awake and brightness is set to maximum automatically.

Does it work offline?

Yes. PickUp Sign works completely offline. No internet connection required. Everything runs locally on your device.

Will my screen stay on?

Yes. Your screen stays awake and brightness is set to maximum while displaying a name. You never need to tap the screen to keep it alive.

How do I change the background colour?

Tap the colour picker icon at the bottom of the screen. Black and white are free. Unlock all 22 colours with the Colour Themes upgrade for $0.99/year.

What languages does it support?

PickUp Sign supports 12 languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Japanese, Korean, Chinese (Simplified and Traditional), Arabic, and Hindi. Smart word-wrap works with all supported scripts.

Is PickUp Sign free?

Yes. The core features (typing a name, full-screen display, screen-awake, and maximum brightness) are completely free. The optional Colour Themes upgrade ($0.99/year) unlocks 22 premium background colours.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open the app, tap the colour picker icon, then tap "Restore Purchases". Your unlock will be restored automatically via your Apple ID.

Does it work on iPad?

Yes. PickUp Sign works on iPad and takes full advantage of the larger screen to display names even bigger and more visibly.

What about Android?

An Android version is coming soon. Sign up for our newsletter or follow us to be the first to know when it launches.

Does PickUp Sign collect my data?

No. PickUp Sign collects absolutely no personal data. Names are stored only on your device and never sent to any server. There are no analytics, no tracking, and no ads. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.

Are there ads?

No. There are no ads in PickUp Sign and there never will be. When you're holding up a sign at an airport, the last thing you need is a banner ad covering someone's name. Your sign is the only thing on screen.

Why not just use another name sign app?

Most name sign apps overlook two critical things: they let your screen dim and lock while you're holding it up, and they don't maximise brightness. PickUp Sign automatically keeps the screen awake and cranks brightness to 100% the moment you display a name. No fiddling with settings, no screen timeout surprises mid-pickup. It also has zero ads. Just the name, full screen.
