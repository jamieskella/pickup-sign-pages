PickUp Sign at London Heathrow Airport (LHR)

Free pickup sign app for chauffeurs and drivers meeting passengers at Heathrow.

Where chauffeurs meet passengers at London Heathrow.

Heathrow does not permit waiting on terminal forecourts. Passenger collection happens in the Short Stay (now "Terminal Parking") car parks attached to each terminal. T2 pickups are at Level 1, T3 at Level 1, T4 at Level 2, T5 at Level 4. Drivers meet passengers on foot at these designated pickup rows after parking. Forecourt waiting attracts an immediate fine.

Practical tips for drivers at LHR.

  • Pre-load the passenger name on the drive in. The Short Stay car parks have weak mobile signal in lower levels.
  • Heathrow is inside London's ULEZ zone, check your vehicle is compliant before driving in.
  • Terminal Parking is signed from the M25 J14 approach. If you miss the Short Stay turn-off, you re-enter via the perimeter road, costing 10 minutes.

Common pickup types at London Heathrow.

  • Business arrivals at T5 from BA transatlantic routes
  • International long-haul at T2 (Star Alliance carriers)
  • Premium chauffeur transfers from T3 (Virgin, oneworld)
