Free pickup sign app for chauffeurs and drivers meeting passengers at Dublin Airport.

Mr Ryan

Where chauffeurs meet passengers
at Dublin Airport.

Dublin Airport has two terminals (T1 and T2), 10 km north of the city via Junction 2 of the M1. Chauffeurs meet passengers in the arrivals hall of the relevant terminal after customs. T2 handles long-haul including Aer Lingus transatlantic, Etihad, and Emirates. T1 handles most short-haul European, Ryanair, and the British Airways shuttle. Drop-off and pickup set-down zones sit in front of both terminals; longer waits use the short-term car parks within a 2 minute walk of either terminal.

Practical tips for DUB.

Set-down or short-stay

Set-down zones are quick only. For meet-and-greet, use the short-term car park and walk in. VIP parking sits 2 minutes from the terminal door.

US preclearance is a quirk

Dublin has US Preclearance for some routes (departing, not arriving). For arriving passengers from the US, customs is still completed at Dublin.

Irish-language names

Pre-load names with fadas (á, é, í, ó, ú) correctly. A digital sign renders them cleanly where a hand-written one often does not.
