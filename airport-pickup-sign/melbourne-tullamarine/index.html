PickUp Sign at
Melbourne Tullamarine
(MEL).

Free pickup sign app for chauffeurs and drivers meeting passengers at Melbourne Airport.

Mr Brown

Where chauffeurs meet passengers
at Melbourne Tullamarine.

Melbourne Airport has four terminals (T1, T2, T3, T4) and chauffeur meeting points are inside each one. T2 International: chauffeurs wait at the chauffeur meeting point between Optus Mobile and Hudson's, accessed via Gate A or Gate B after customs. T1 Qantas Domestic: meeting point in front of baggage carousel 1, near car rentals (gates 1 to 12) or in front of the relevant carousel (gates 13 to 24). T3 Virgin and Rex: next to Hudson's Cafe near the baggage carousel exit. T4 Jetstar: in front of the cafe area at the base of the escalators before baggage claim.

Practical tips for MEL.

Meet inside, not at the kerb

All four terminals have designated chauffeur meeting points inside. Kerbside pickup is restricted and busy.

T123 Transport Hub coming

From Spring 2026, T1, T2, T3 pickup and drop-off shifts to the new elevated T123 Transport Hub. Confirm the current layout before each pickup.

Free Wait Zone

Use the airport's free Wait Zone if you arrive early. Saves circling and saves parking fees.

Common pickup types.

International long-haul, T2

Emirates, Qatar, Singapore Airlines arrivals. Chauffeur waiting between Optus and Hudson's with the name up.

Premium business, T1

Qantas domestic transfer for a corporate client. Driver at the carousel 1 meeting point, sign visible from the escalators down.

Budget-tier arrival, T4

Jetstar family arrival, driver waiting in front of the cafe area with the family name displayed.

Is PickUp Sign free to use?

Yes. The app is free forever on iPhone, iPad, and Android. No ads, no trials. Optional Colour Themes upgrade is US$0.99/year for premium background colours.

Does it work without internet at the airport?

Yes. The app runs entirely offline. No mobile data, no Wi-Fi required.

Will the screen stay awake while I hold it up?

Yes. PickUp Sign keeps the screen on at maximum brightness automatically while a name is displayed.
