PickUp Sign at John F. Kennedy International Airport (JFK)

Free pickup sign app for chauffeurs and drivers meeting passengers at JFK.

Where chauffeurs meet passengers at New York JFK.

Professional car service chauffeurs at JFK meet passengers inside the terminal at baggage claim level, holding a name sign. Different terminals have different meeting points: T1 baggage claim at the base of customs escalators, T4 arrivals hall near carousels 10-13 (most international arrivals), T5 (JetBlue) baggage claim, T7 baggage claim at customs exit, T8 (American) baggage claim near the main corridor. Uber and Lyft do not pick up inside terminals at JFK, only car services do.

Practical tips for drivers at JFK.

  • T4 has the heaviest international volume. Customs queues vary, give yourself a buffer.
  • Domestic arrivals get 30 minutes complimentary wait, international gets 45 minutes per most car-service standards.
  • The Authorized Vehicle Area (AVA) is where commercial drivers queue, not the terminals themselves.

Common pickup types at New York JFK.

  • International business arrivals at T4 (Delta, Emirates, BA)
  • Domestic and Caribbean JetBlue at T5
  • American Airlines transatlantic at T8
