PickUp Sign at Rome Fiumicino Airport (FCO)

App gratuita per cartelli di accoglienza per chauffeur al Fiumicino.

Where chauffeurs meet passengers at Rome Fiumicino.

At Fiumicino, private chauffeurs meet passengers in the Main Waiting Hall after the customs exit, never at the kerbside (kerbside pickup is prohibited at FCO). The designated pickup area is marked by tall round columns labelled "LIMO DRIVER" and "N.C.C." on top. Most international flights arrive at Terminal 3; KLM, AirFrance and domestic Italian flights arrive at Terminal 1.

Practical tips for drivers at FCO.

  • Driver vehicles park in the designated NCC area across the street near the main parking garages, a 3-5 minute walk from the hall.
  • Pickup is never at the kerb. Forecourt waiting attracts a fine.
  • Allow ~40 minutes after scheduled landing for customs and baggage before the passenger reaches the Main Waiting Hall.

Common pickup types at Rome Fiumicino.

  • Long-haul international arrivals at T3 (Lufthansa, Emirates, ITA, others)
  • Schengen and domestic at T1 (KLM, AirFrance, ITA domestic)
  • Premium meet-and-greet for ITA Airways VIP service
