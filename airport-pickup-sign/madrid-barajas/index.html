PickUp Sign at Madrid Adolfo Suárez Barajas Airport (MAD)

App gratis para carteles de recogida para chóferes en Barajas.

Where chauffeurs meet passengers at Madrid Barajas.

At MAD, chauffeurs wait in the arrivals lounge holding a name board after baggage claim and customs. Terminal pickup points differ: T1 drivers wait at the Post Office, T2 drivers wait at the Aena Information Point under the green umbrella, T4 drivers wait at the Arrival Lounge near Bar Bareto Madrid. T4 handles most long-haul international.

Practical tips for drivers at MAD.

  • MAD has 4 active terminals (T1, T2, T3, T4) plus T4S satellite. Always confirm the exact terminal at booking.
  • Long-haul arrivals via T4 take longer through customs than short-haul Schengen via T1, plan accordingly.
  • Madrid-Barajas to Madrid CBD is ~20 minutes off-peak via the M-11/M-30.

Common pickup types at Madrid Barajas.

  • Iberia and oneworld international at T4
  • Star Alliance and skyteam international at T1
  • Schengen short-haul at T2 and T3
