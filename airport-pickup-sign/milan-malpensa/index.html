Where chauffeurs meet passengers
at Milan Malpensa.

Malpensa has two separated terminals (T1 and T2). Chauffeurs meet passengers at the designated driver meeting point just outside Arrivals in each terminal. T1 has a 15-minute Area subject to electronic plate-recognition and time-limited access. Chauffeur car rental companies access dedicated Level A (prepaid card) or Level B (Telepass) areas. There is a free 60-minute waiting area between the two terminals if you are early.

Practical tips for MXP.

Use the free 60-minute area if early

Exceed 15 minutes in the T1 short-stay zone and expect automated fines. The free area between T1 and T2 has no such limit.

T1 and T2 are not walkable

Connected by shuttle bus, not on foot. Confirm your terminal before driving in.

Plan ~50 minutes to central Milan

Malpensa-to-central-Milan is approximately 50 km, around 50 minutes in light traffic.

Common pickup types.

Long-haul intercontinental, T1

Star Alliance, Emirates, Cathay Pacific. Larger, slower customs.

Low-cost European, T2

easyJet, Ryanair. Faster turnarounds.

Premium business transfer

To Milan CBD or to Como/Lake Maggiore; clear name sign matters when picking up unfamiliar clients.
