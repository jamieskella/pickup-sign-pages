PickUp Sign at Milan Malpensa Airport (MXP)

App gratuita per cartelli di accoglienza per chauffeur al Malpensa.

Where chauffeurs meet passengers at Milan Malpensa.

Malpensa has two separated terminals (T1 and T2). Chauffeurs meet passengers at the designated driver meeting point just outside Arrivals in each terminal. T1 has a 15-minute Area subject to electronic plate-recognition and time-limited access. Chauffeur car rental companies access dedicated Level A (prepaid card) or Level B (Telepass) areas. There is a free 60-minute waiting area between the two terminals if you are early.

Practical tips for drivers at MXP.

  • If you exceed 15 minutes in the T1 short-stay zone, expect automated fines. Use the free 60-minute waiting area between T1 and T2 instead.
  • T1 and T2 are connected by shuttle bus, not walkable. Confirm your terminal before driving in.
  • Malpensa-to-central-Milan is ~50 km, ~50 minutes in light traffic.

Common pickup types at Milan Malpensa.

  • Long-haul intercontinental at T1 (Star Alliance, Emirates, Cathay Pacific)
  • Low-cost European at T2 (easyJet, Ryanair)
  • Premium business transfers to Milan CBD or Como/Lake Maggiore
