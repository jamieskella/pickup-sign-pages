PickUp Sign at Barcelona El Prat Airport (BCN)

App gratis para carteles de recogida para chóferes en El Prat.

Where chauffeurs meet passengers at Barcelona El Prat.

At BCN, private drivers are not permitted to enter the baggage claim area itself. There is a designated waiting area in the arrivals hall where drivers wait with a name sign after the passenger exits customs and baggage claim. T1 handles most international and Schengen long-haul; T2 handles low-cost and regional.

Practical tips for drivers at BCN.

  • Drivers wait outside baggage claim, never inside. Communicate with the passenger by phone or messaging once they have cleared customs.
  • BCN T1 is significantly larger than T2, expect longer walks for both passenger and driver coordination.
  • Barcelona city centre is ~15 minutes from BCN off-peak via the C-31.

Common pickup types at Barcelona El Prat.

  • Long-haul international at T1 (Iberia, Lufthansa, oneworld, Star Alliance)
  • Low-cost European at T2 (Ryanair, Vueling, easyJet)
  • Cruise port transfers from BCN to the Port of Barcelona
