Where chauffeurs meet passengers
at Barcelona El Prat.

At BCN, private drivers are not permitted to enter the baggage claim area itself. There is a designated waiting area in the arrivals hall where drivers wait with a name sign after the passenger exits customs and baggage claim. T1 handles most international and Schengen long-haul; T2 handles low-cost and regional.

Practical tips for BCN.

Wait outside baggage claim

Drivers wait outside the baggage claim, never inside. Communicate with the passenger by phone or messaging once they have cleared customs.

T1 is bigger

BCN T1 is significantly larger than T2. Expect longer walks for both passenger and driver coordination.

Quick to central Barcelona

Around 15 minutes from BCN off-peak via the C-31.

Common pickup types.

Long-haul international, T1

Iberia, Lufthansa, oneworld, Star Alliance long-haul.

Low-cost European, T2

Ryanair, Vueling, easyJet. Faster turnaround, less premium expectation.

Cruise port transfer

BCN to the Port of Barcelona for cruise departures; family/group pickups common.

Yes. The app runs entirely offline. No mobile data, no Wi-Fi required.

Yes. PickUp Sign keeps the screen on at maximum brightness automatically while a name is displayed.
