Where chauffeurs meet passengers
at Singapore Changi.

Changi has clearly signposted Meet & Greet zones in each terminal's arrivals meeting hall (T1, T2, T3, T4). Drivers meet passengers inside the meeting hall after baggage claim. Waiting kerbside or at the arrival level is restricted, fines are enforced. Multi-storey car park pickup areas allow longer waits without time pressure.

Practical tips for SIN.

Use the meeting hall, not the kerb

The kerb has a zigzag no-stopping line. Fines are issued routinely.

Multi-storey if you are early

There is no time restriction in the multi-storey car park, only the parking fee.

Confirm the terminal in advance

T4 services are subject to additional surcharges compared to T1, T2, T3 for some pickup services.

Common pickup types.
