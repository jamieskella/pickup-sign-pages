PickUp Sign at Singapore Changi Airport (SIN)

Free pickup sign app for chauffeurs and drivers meeting passengers at Changi.

Where chauffeurs meet passengers at Singapore Changi.

Changi has clearly signposted Meet & Greet zones in each terminal's arrivals meeting hall (T1, T2, T3, T4). Drivers meet passengers inside the meeting hall after baggage claim. Waiting kerbside or at the arrival level is restricted, fines are enforced. Multi-storey car park pickup areas allow longer waits without time pressure.

Practical tips for drivers at SIN.

  • Arrive at the airport-side Meet & Greet zone, not the kerb. The kerb has a zigzag no-stopping line, fines are issued routinely.
  • If you are early, wait in the multi-storey car park. There is no time restriction there, only the parking fee.
  • T4 services are subject to additional surcharges compared to T1, T2, T3 for some pickup services. Confirm terminal in advance.

Common pickup types at Singapore Changi.

  • Premium chauffeur pickup for Singapore Airlines arrivals at T3
  • MICE event delegate fleet greeting at Marina Bay Sands transfers
  • Tourism inbound from regional Southeast Asia at T2
