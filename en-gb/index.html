Frequently asked.

Is PickUp Sign free in United Kingdom?

Yes. The core app is free forever in United Kingdom and everywhere. There are no ads, no trials, no time limits. The optional Colour Themes upgrade is US$0.99 per year if you want premium background colours.

Does it work without internet at the airport?

Yes. PickUp Sign is fully offline. No mobile data, no Wi-Fi, no airport network required. Type the name, hold the screen up.

What devices does it run on?

iPhone, iPad, and Android phones and tablets. Free on the App Store and Google Play.

Will my screen stay awake while I am holding it up?

Yes. The app keeps the screen on at maximum brightness automatically the entire time a name is displayed.
